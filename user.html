Explore Each Strand

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Our STEM strand offers state-of-the-art laboratories, robotics workshops, and advanced math courses. Students participate in science fairs, coding bootcamps, and engineering projects that prepare them for technical fields.

  • Modern science laboratories
  • Robotics and coding classes
  • Math Olympiad training
  • Research immersion programs

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

The ABM strand provides hands-on business simulations, accounting software training, and entrepreneurship seminars. Students manage real projects and take part in business competitions.

  • Business plan competitions
  • Accounting and finance workshops
  • Entrepreneurship mentoring
  • Industry internships

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

HUMSS students enjoy debate clubs, creative writing workshops, and immersion in community development projects. We nurture leaders, communicators, and advocates for social change.

  • Debate and journalism clubs
  • Community service programs
  • Psychology and sociology classes
  • Creative writing workshops

TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood)

TVL provides hands-on training in technical fields such as cookery, electrical installation, and information technology. Graduates are ready for employment or further specialization.

  • NCII certification courses
  • Kitchen and workshop facilities
  • IT and computer servicing
  • Aquaculture and Processing

PBM (Pre-Baccalaureate Maritime)

Our PBM strand prepares students for maritime careers with courses in navigation, seamanship, and safety. Experience ship bridge simulators and field trips to maritime institutions.

  • Ship bridge simulator training
  • Maritime safety and navigation
  • Marine engineering basics
  • Partnerships with maritime schools

SMAW (Shield Manufacturing and Welding)

Focuses on shield manufacturing and welding, providing students with practical skills in metal fabrication and construction.

  • Shield manufacturing techniques
  • Welding and metal fabrication
  • Construction safety protocols
  • Industrial design and engineering
